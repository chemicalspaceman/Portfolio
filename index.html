Game Development



Sector 7 is a two-player information based abstract strategy board game by designed me. A friend (Jon Pennant) helped me turn the game into a web app using Node.js and pure JS for the frontend so that it can be played over the web. Click below to "Play Online" or view the rules here.

Description

On the 4 orbits around a pink planet, both players place four/five ships, the goal of the game is to remove all of the opponent's ships.

Each player holds three cards. There are a further three cards face up in the center that each display possible movements. On a player's turn they can choose one of the cards from the center and: move one of their pieces on its orbit to the sector indicated by the chosen card, then replace the card with a card from their hand; move a moon along its orbit by the number of spaces indicated by the chosen card; or move one ship to an adjacent space. These actions may not be repeated until all possible action types have been completed.

Moving a ship onto one of the opponent's ships removes that ship from the game, as does hitting an opponent's ship with a moon. Players can regain a ship if they occupy a moon as it moves into sector 7. Removing all of the opponent's ships wins the game.
